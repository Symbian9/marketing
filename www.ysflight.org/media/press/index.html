Top Free Flight Simulator: YSFlight

February 8, 2007

Of all the free flight simulator games here, this one impresses me the most because it was created by just one guy, who happens to also be a pilot. The best part about this game is that you can use it as a flight simulation trainer, or you can use it as an air combat game! It is a relatively small download, the plane choices are actually planes you’d want to fly, and it’s just plain fun. YSFlight simply wins this contest hands down!

Game review: YS Flight Simulator

April 8, 2013

YSflight is a free flight simulator that is made for Windows, Mac and even Linux. YSflight is not very dependent on RAM and graphics cards so it will run on most, if not all, computers. YSflight is ideal for first time ‘flight simmers’ and is quite good for just wanting to fly a plane without having to buy all the software that a hardcore simmer would have to buy for FSX or X-Plane.
